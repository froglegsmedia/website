How We Work

We Handle
Everything.

Most productions are a relay race — creative over here, production over there, post somewhere else entirely. By the time it's done, the original idea is unrecognizable. We keep the whole race in-house. Same team, same vision, start to finish.

The Advantage

Why Frog Legs
Just Works.

01
We speak agency.

Tight briefs. Fast turnarounds. Revisions without a meltdown. We've been a production partner to agencies long enough to know exactly what you need — and what you definitely don't have time for.

02
We speak brand.

No agency markup. No middleman translating your vision. You talk to the people actually making it. The idea that walks in is the idea that ends up on screen.

03
The gear is serious.

ARRI, RED, Sony Cinema Line. Licensed drone operators. Full lighting packages. Steadicam, gimbal, whatever the shot needs. No compromises on the technical side — ever.

04
The crew is better.

We don't staff up with whoever's available. Our crew is hand-picked — the people we'd want on our own set. DPs, ACs, gaffers, sound — everyone here is genuinely good at their job.
